Dominic Fike

Early Career

  • 2017: Begins releasing music on SoundCloud from his home in Naples, Florida.
  • 2018 (April): Drops Don’t Forget About Me, Demos on SoundCloud while under house arrest.
  • 2018 (August): Signs a $4 million deal with Columbia Records after a label bidding war.
  • 2018 (October): Official re-release of Don’t Forget About Me, Demos under Columbia.

Rise to Fame

  • “3 Nights” becomes a viral hit, charting internationally and earning radio play.
  • Features on Brockhampton’s “Sugar” (Remix) and collaborations begin picking up.
  • Performs on late-night shows and major festivals, including Lollapalooza.

Debut Album

  • July 2020: Releases debut studio album What Could Possibly Go Wrong.
  • Singles include: “Chicken Tenders,” “Politics & Violence,” “Vampire.”
  • Album charts on Billboard 200; receives mixed to positive critical acclaim.
  • Collaborates with Justin Bieber on “Die for You (Remix).”

Breaking Into Acting

  • 2021 (Dec): Announced as a cast member for Season 2 of HBO’s Euphoria.
  • 2022 (Jan–Feb): Debuts as Elliot on Euphoria; releases “Elliot’s Song” with Zendaya.
  • Gains critical praise and a larger fan base through the show.

Second Album & More

  • April–July 2023: Releases singles “Dancing in the Courthouse,” “Ant Pile,” “Mona Lisa,” “Mama’s Boy.”
  • July 7, 2023: Releases second album Sunburn.
  • Themes include his upbringing, family, and fame.
  • Debuts at #30 on the Billboard 200.

Diversification

  • 2023: Stars in Earth Mama, his feature film debut as “Miles.”
  • Continues touring and festival appearances post-Sunburn.
  • 2024 (April): Releases 14 Minutes EP — a raw, stripped-back project.
  • 2024: Appears in indie film Little Death as AJ.

Ongoing Work

  • Early 2025: Releases “Love Hangover” with BLACKPINK’s Jennie.
  • Charts on Billboard Hot 100 (#96), marks his first major K-pop crossover.
  • Expected to return in Euphoria Season 3 (TBA).
